Expression : The logical expression entered into the text field.

Variable: Any single alphabetic character is a variable.

Constant: True or False, May also be represented as 1 or 0.

Operator: AND(∧), OR(∨), NOT(¬), IMPLIES(→), IFF(↔)

Batch Mode: The expression is not evaluated until the ”Evaluate” button is pressed.

Dynamic Mode: The expression is evaluate each time it changes.

Full Mode: The table is displayed variables first and then each step in the correct order of operations.

Compact Mode: The expression is displayed with the results of each variable, constant, and operator for every step in a column under itself. Also known as the Half-as-Fast method.
